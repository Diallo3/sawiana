Wind Power

Small Wind, Big Power

Next-generation turbines individually generate 2 to 5 KW, 10 and 20 KW systems available soon, using only a 9m (30 ft) tower and 3.5m (12ft) wingspan.

A typical 12 x 5 KW turbine microgrid is linearly scalable and can power dozens of apartments, a village, large office park, or industrial application. However, specific physical layouts have shown geometric increase in power generation, driving even more efficiency to meet your power needs.
