Small Wind, Big Power

Each turbine individually generates 2 or 5 KW/hr, with 10 and 20 KW/HR systems available soon.

A typical 12 x 5 KW turbine microgrid is linearly scalable and can power dozens of apartments, a village, large office park, or industrial application. However, specific physical layouts have shown geometric increase in power generation.
